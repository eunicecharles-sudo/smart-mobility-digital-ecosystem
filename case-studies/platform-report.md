
📌 **Project Overview**

The Smart Mobility & Digital Ecosystem is a multi-service platform that integrates ride-hailing, driver operations, financial services, telecom services, rewards systems, vehicle fleet management, and external energy infrastructure partnerships (EV charging and CNG stations).
The platform is designed as a multi-sided mobility ecosystem, connecting riders, drivers, administrators, and external service providers in a unified system.


🎯 **Project Objectives**

The main objectives of the platform are:

- Provide seamless ride-hailing services for riders
- Enable efficient driver management and earnings tracking
- Support multi-payment systems including cash, bank transfer, internal coins, and Pi cryptocurrency
- Integrate external EV charging and CNG station partners for energy access
- Introduce a reward-based engagement system (Internal coin)
- Offer telecom services such as airtime and data purchases
- Provide centralized monitoring through an Admin Dashboard
  
🧩 **System Architecture Overview**

The platform consists of the following modules:

- 🏠 Landing Page Website

🔄 **User Flow**

🚗 Rider Flow

1. User accesses Rider App
2. Selects ride type
3. Books a ride
4. Chooses payment method:
   - Cash
   - Bank Transfer 
   - Internal coin
   - Pi cryptocurrency
5. Completes trip
6. Earns coin rewards based on engagement rules

🚕 Driver Flow

1. Driver accesses Driver App
2. Accepts ride requests
3. Completes trip
4. Earnings are credited to wallet
5. Uses EV or CNG partner stations for energy needs
6. Tracks performance via SideCar review system

🎁 **Pepcoin Reward System**

Pepcoin is the internal platform’s engagement and loyalty currency.

Key Features:

- Users earn Pepcoin after completing up to five rides
- Rewards are distributed based on engagement milestones
- Promo coins are issued for campaigns and user engagement
- Promo codes are used for retention and acquisition strategies
